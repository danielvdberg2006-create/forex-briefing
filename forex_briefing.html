AOI Detection Method: Scans Weekly (2 years) and Daily (2 years) separately for rejection zones with a minimum of 3 touches. A valid touch is any market structure point (swing high or low) whose candle body closes within or touches the zone and then rejects back out — candles that close through and continue are not counted. Zone size is capped at 60 pips. Weekly and Daily zones within 30 pips of each other are combined into one stronger confluence zone. All zones within the largest trend structure are shown — including those beyond current price — to support break and retest setups. Max lookback: 2 years Daily · 2 years Weekly.
